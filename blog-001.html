How much material?

Length and width measurements with the filter

First measurement

Start with a 11 inch by 16 inch piece of cotton material and an 8 inch square poly rayon filter (esthetic wipes). Measure along the 11 inches and cut at 8 inches. Save the excess 3 inches of material to make the side pieces. Fold the 16 inch side in half to make 8 inches. Press off.

Sew an Edge

It's a simple bias stitch

Sewing

I measured apprx 1/4 inch ran a straight stitch.

Flipping the Material

Turning the material

Flip it

Turn the material right-side-out and press off at the seam.

Pleating

Measuring pleats

Pleat measurement

Measure down 1 1/4 inch, fold and press. Measure down 1/2 inch, fold in opposite direction and press. Repeat the process.

Sewing Pleats

Keeping the pleats in place

Sewing the pleats

Run a straight stitch along either side measuring in appx 1/2 inch.

Sides

Made out of the excess 3 inches cut off

Side measurements

Cut two pieces 5 inches long.

Side Measurements

Steps for sides

Measurements

Measure to top/bottom edges down 1/2 inch. Fold, press off and straight stitch. Next, press the other sides 1/2 inch. Then fold in half long ways.

Checking the Fit

Incorporating the pieces

Measurements

Lay the body inside lining up the edges.

Putting it Together

Sew it Together

Sewing the Sides

Measure to top/bottom edges down 1/2 inch. Fold, press off and straight stitch. Next, press the other sides 1/2 inch. Then fold in half long ways. Sew a straight stitch along both sides, remembering to back-stitch 5 stitches.

Nearly Finished

It's about fortification

Zig-Zag stitch

Sew a zig-zag along sides to fortify the sides.

Last Step

Running the drawstring

Finished product

Feed a 54 inch flat shoestring through the sides.
